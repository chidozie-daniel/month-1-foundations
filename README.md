This repo shows and represents my learning journey as a full stack developer. Not judging myself, just being honest and allowing myself to be 
a beginner once and for all. One problem with university is that it throws random things at you. You switch too much between languages,
you attempt to learn "everything" even when it's impossible. A degree gives you safety, but you're learning comes from what you do on your own.

Just a word of advice for anyone:
"If you don't allow yourself to become a beginner, you'll never stop being one"
